Outsourcing/Offshoring

Rackemail supports outsourcing and offshoring companies by streamlining client communication, managing project updates, and improving internal coordination.

Benefits for Outsourcing/Offshoring Industry

  • Client Communication:
    • Maintain regular communication with clients about project updates and deliverables. Automate follow-ups to ensure clients are kept informed.
    • Use hyper-personalization to address clients by name and reference their specific project requirements.
  • Project Management:
    • Send automated updates and reports to clients about project progress. Use personalized emails to provide detailed insights and gather feedback.
    • Manage all project-related communication from a unified inbox to ensure timely responses.
  • Internal Coordination:
    • Improve internal communication among team members. Manage all internal emails from a unified inbox to ensure everyone is updated about project status.
    • Share DND and bounce lists to maintain clean and effective communication channels.
