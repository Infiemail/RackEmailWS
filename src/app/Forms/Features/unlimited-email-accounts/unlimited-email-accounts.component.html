Unlimited Email Accounts: Seamless Integration and Management

With Rackemail, you have the freedom to connect and manage multiple email accounts without any additional costs. This feature is designed to streamline your email marketing efforts and ensure you have a flexible and efficient system for all your communication needs.

Seamless Email Integrations

Rackemail supports a wide range of email services, ensuring compatibility and ease of use:
  • Gmail & G-Suite: Connect your Gmail or G-Suite accounts effortlessly. Benefit from Google's robust infrastructure while utilizing Rackemail's advanced email marketing capabilities.
  • Outlook & Office 365: Integrate with Outlook and Office 365 to leverage Microsoft's trusted email services within the Rackemail platform.
  • Hotmail & More: Rackemail also supports older email services like Hotmail, ensuring you can bring all your accounts into one cohesive system.
  • IMAP & SMTP: For other email providers, Rackemail offers integration via IMAP & SMTP, providing flexibility and ensuring that no matter your email provider, Rackemail has you covered.

Benefits of Unlimited Email Accounts

Rackemail supports a wide range of email services, ensuring compatibility and ease of use:
  • Centralized Management: Handle all your email accounts from one centralized location. No more logging in and out of multiple email services; Rackemail brings everything together under one roof.
  • Increased Sending Limits: With multiple accounts, you can distribute your email campaigns across various addresses, ensuring higher deliverability rates and avoiding sending limits imposed by individual providers.
  • Enhanced Organization: Keep your work, personal, and marketing emails separate yet accessible. Rackemail's interface allows you to switch between accounts seamlessly.
  • Improved Deliverability: By rotating between different email accounts, you can reduce the risk of your emails being marked as spam, ensuring better deliverability and higher open rates.

How to Connect Your Accounts

  • Sign Up or Log In: Start by signing up for Rackemail or logging into your existing account.
  • Access Settings: Navigate to the settings section where you will find the email accounts management tab.
  • Add Account: Click on 'Add Account' and follow the prompts to integrate your email accounts. Rackemail's guided setup makes the process quick and easy.
  • Verification: Verify your email accounts as prompted to ensure they are ready for use within the Rackemail platform.

This feature empowers you to handle larger campaigns, maintain higher deliverability rates, and manage your communications more efficiently than ever before.
